# Code of Conduct

## The rule

Treat everyone in this project's issues, pull requests, and discussions
the way you'd want to be treated by a senior engineer on your best day:
**directly about the code, respectfully about the person**.

Disagreement is welcome — it's how we find better designs. Personal
attacks, harassment, sustained disruption, or discrimination of any
kind are not.

## Reporting

If you see something that crosses the line, email `hello@localkin.ai`
with the issue/PR/comment URL and a short description. Reports are
confidential, handled by the maintainer, and resolved within 7 days.

## Enforcement

Graduated, proportional:
1. **Note**: private message explaining what was out of line.
2. **Warning**: public comment referencing this doc, blocking further
   escalation.
3. **Temporary ban**: issue/PR interaction removed for 30 days.
4. **Permanent ban**: repeated or severe violations.

Based on the
[Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/),
trimmed to what we'll actually enforce.
